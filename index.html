Halifax

Date: 2023-04-12
Duration: 4 days

Summary

Morris Lake: Found between Portland Estates and Caldwell Road is a 3.5km long lake, Morris Lake is a close to home paddling option. Public boat launches at each end of the lake make it easy to access the water.
Summer canoe rentals are available from Kiwanis Beach off of Caldwell Road.

Lake Banook and Mic Mac: Banook and Mic Mac lakes are separated by a causeway (Highway 111). These freshwater lakes are a haven for serious canoe and kayak training.
